Some SeaDuke samples have a module to use pass the ticket with Kerberos for authentication.
Mimikatz’s <code>LSADUMP::DCSync</code>, <code>KERBEROS::Golden</code>, and <code>KERBEROS::PTT</code> modules implement the three steps required to extract the krbtgt account hash and create/use Kerberos tickets.
