Reg may be used to find credentials in the Windows Registry.
PowerSploit has several modules that search the Windows Registry for stored credentials: <code>Get-UnattendedInstallFile</code>, <code>Get-Webconfig</code>, <code>Get-ApplicationHost</code>, <code>Get-SiteListPassword</code>, <code>Get-CachedGPPPassword</code>, and <code>Get-RegistryAutoLogon</code>.
